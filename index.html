Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right course for me?

Discover your passions, strengths, and long-term goals. Explore various subjects, consider practicality, and envision your future. Seek advice, reflect on personal interests, and choose a course aligned with your aspirations and potential.

What programs and courses are offered?

We offer comprehensive programs and courses in UI/UX design, web design, mobile app design, user research, prototyping, and design thinking, empowering students to excel in diverse design disciplines.

Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in your courses?

No prerequisites are required for enrollment. Our courses welcome beginners and professionals alike, providing comprehensive instruction and support to students at all levels of expertise in design.

What software or tools will I need for these courses?

Our courses typically utilize industry-standard tools such as Adobe XD, Sketch, Figma, and InVision for UI/UX design. Access to a computer with internet connectivity is essential for completing coursework effectively.

How long are the courses?

Course durations vary depending on the program and specific course. Typically, our courses range from 6 weeks to 6 months, providing comprehensive instruction and ample time for skill development and project completion.

Are there any hands-on projects or assignments in the courses?

Yes, our courses feature hands-on projects and assignments designed to enhance practical skills and reinforce theoretical concepts. Students engage in real-world projects to build a robust portfolio and gain practical experience.

Can I get a certificate upon completing the courses?

Yes, upon successfully completing our courses, you will receive a certificate of completion. This certificate verifies your proficiency in UI design and enhances your credibility in the field.

Do you offer mentorship or career counseling for the students?

Yes, we offer mentorship and career counseling to our students. Our experienced mentors provide guidance, feedback, and support to help students navigate their career paths and achieve their professional goals effectively.

How can I access support if I have technical difficulties during the courses?

You can access technical support through our online platform, where dedicated support staff are available to assist you with any technical difficulties you encounter during the courses.

Can I access course materials and lectures after completing the program?

Yes, you will have continued access to course materials and lectures after completing the program. This ensures that you can review content and reference materials as needed for ongoing learning and reference.
